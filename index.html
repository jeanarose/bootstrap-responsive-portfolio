About Me

My name is Jeana Rose and I am a recent graduate from the University of Georgia under the College of Public Health. I knew when I graduated that I did not want to pursue a career in public health. I was lucky enough to have a connection who offered me a job in the tech world, and it was there that I gained exposure to software development. Through my experience over the last year, I've grown excited about a career in software development and knew I had to invest in myself to make that a possiblity.

I recently got married and moved to Midtown Atlanta with my husband. We own a Golden Retriever named Moose who is currently six months old. I enjoy taking long walks, exercising, training Moose, and spending time with friends and family. An interesting fact about me is that I was on drumline for five years: two seasons on bass drum, two season on snare drum, and one season on cymbals.
